# Bird's Eye executive value architecture

## Product objective

Canary should help a communicator move from media to local decisions, then convert approved work and evidence into a concise leadership artifact. Bird's Eye View is the executive proof layer that supports sales and retention. It is not the working queue.

## Three layers

### 1. Communications work layer

Used by communications staff:

- Unified Review Queue for news and social
- Review-only recommendations
- Ownership, due dates, status, and verification facts
- Notes and approved completion outcomes
- Daily digest links into the filtered queue

### 2. Trust and governance layer

Explains why the evidence is credible:

- Strategic-profile confidence, active priorities, source documents, and last review date
- Collection freshness and provider health
- Public-data coverage boundaries
- Human approval and source verification

### 3. Executive proof layer

Bird's Eye View should show only approved, leadership-safe evidence:

- Total mentions and Canary Score
- Strategic Hits and percentage
- Earned Media and percentage
- Strategic focus-area coverage
- Source-governance summary
- Approved action/outcome counts and selected outcome evidence in a later appendix
- CSV and PDF exports

## Guardrails

- Do not show draft recommendations in Bird's Eye View.
- Do not expose internal notes, raw AI reasoning, unresolved facts, or private provider diagnostics to executives.
- Only completed and explicitly approved outcomes may bubble into the executive appendix.
- Keep denominators, filters, date ranges, source confidence, and freshness visible enough to support trust.
- Preserve district scoping in every work, trust, and executive query.
- Public social discovery is useful but incomplete and must not be represented as the complete native Meta inbox.

## Delivery order

1. Strategic Alignment governance and provenance in Bird's Eye View.
2. Unified Review Queue with persistent action state.
3. Collection freshness and provider health.
4. Daily communicator digest.
5. Approved leadership action/outcome appendix.
